NHS Consultation Risk Tracker — Read-only view

This dashboard tracks 32 NHS service reconfiguration proposals across 26 ICB footprints in England, monitoring compliance with Section 244 consultation obligations under the NHS Act 2006.

Data is updated by the NHS England Service Reconfiguration Directorate team using the Claude.ai dashboard. This page reads from shared storage and reflects the latest saved results automatically.

Live data — updates automatically when the team saves new results

Risk levels

Critical / NOW — S244 obligation at immediate risk. Action required before any service change takes place.

High / NEAR — Significant risk within 12 months. Active monitoring and early engagement required.

Medium / FAR — Risk identified but not imminent. Monitor and reassess quarterly.

Closed — Precedent case. Process concluded but outcome informs approach to live proposals.

Sources monitored

For each proposal the tracker checks: trust board papers, HOSC and joint scrutiny committee minutes, ICB board publications, CQC inspection reports and enforcement notices, parliamentary written questions, IRP activity, and news and press releases.

For questions about specific proposals contact the NHS England Service Reconfiguration Directorate. For technical issues with this dashboard contact the team directly.
